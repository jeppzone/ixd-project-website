Farmkampen

WHAT?

A fast-paced tablet game where a player is tasked with expanding a farm by cutting down trees and using resources. The end goal is to generate as much food as possible in a limitied amount of time.

WHY?

This is a different and hopefully engaging way of learing the causes and effects of rainforest deforestation.
